Associate Partners

NITE involves collaboration with a number of Associate Partners with whom we will be devising an ambitious programme of community co-designed cultural events and activities. Our aim is to map key public spaces, experiences and narratives of night-time cultures and to contribute to policy approaches to night-time cultures and economies, releasing the potential night spaces offer in creating more inclusive cities.

Activities and outputs will include roundtables and workshops and documentary film production. The projectâs website will open up new spaces for dialogue and exchange of ideas and best practices between migrant communities, local authorities, and the broader public. With migration a defining characteristic of contemporary urban life, key and often polarizing in current policy, political and public debates in Europe, NITE aims to support community wellbeing and better integration at local, national and transnational levels.
